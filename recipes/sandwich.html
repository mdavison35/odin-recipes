Sandwich

A timeless classic. Take two pieces of breach and butter one side of each piece. Then add a layer of peanut butter over the butter. Lastly, place the two peanut buttered sides on top of each other and enjoy

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each piece of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter over each buttered side
  3. Place the two buttered sides on top of each other
  4. Enjoy!
