Lasanga Recipe

Description

Lasanga is an all time favorite! Start by boiling the water and adding the lasanga sheeets. Next create the pasta sauce using fresh or canned tomatoes. Cook the mince and layer the mince, pasta and sauce and top with cheese.

Once the lasanga is put together, throw in the oven and wait 60 minutes. Let it cool for another 60 minutes and you're ready to serve!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
